EARTH
The Fall

Adam sinned against God.
And because of that one sin,
all mankind was separated from Him.

“But your iniquities have separated between you and your God, and your sins have hid his face from you.”

Isaiah 59:2
The Gift

But God did not leave us.
He sent His Son, born of Mary,
to heal what sin had broken.

“Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners.”

1 Timothy 1:15
Confession

When we confess our sin, we are redeemed —
for Jesus died and rose again,
and in Him we have eternal life.

“For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.”

John 3:16
